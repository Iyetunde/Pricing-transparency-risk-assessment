Risk Management Implications

The results show that pricing generally follows logical patterns based on service level and demand. However, prices are more unstable in short booking windows and in economy class. These areas may need closer monitoring.

From a governance perspective, this highlights the need for:

Regular monitoring of areas where price changes are more unpredictable

Using data to support risk ratings

Making oversight decisions based on evidence rather than assumptions
